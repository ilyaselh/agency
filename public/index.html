About us

We are specialists in Arabic translation

EA Translations was designed to serve the fast-growing Arabic industry and fulfil the need for efficient communications on the Arabic market. More businesses than ever are actively trying to reach wider audiences in the Arab world, and numerous organizations are collaborating with Arab bodies every day to expand their reach and activities.

EA Translations brings together a team of proficient native Arabic translators, who are united by their love of Arabic language and their mastery of other foreign languages.

Our team is here to help you break language barriers to effectively communicate and understand others.

We aim to help you reach peaks of linguistic mountains and take the plunge from a monolingual shore to a multilingual ocean.

English into Arabic translations

As a team of Arabic natives and qualified translators, we aim to provide you with professional Arabic translation services finished to a very high standard. We ensure the delivery of accurate, sophisticated, and smooth final product in Arabic, free from dialectal phrases and common errors.

Arabic into English translations

If you require translation services from Arabic into English, we collaborate with native speakers of English to ensure your translation is on the highest level. Work into or from other languages can be provided upon request.

Arabic dialects

If you are looking for Arabic Dialects, such as Moroccan Darija, Egyptian, or Lebanese, you are in safe hands. Our Arabian team can cater for your needs and deliver your translation in the dialect required.
